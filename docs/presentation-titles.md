# Conference Presentation Titles for Microbots

## Recommended Title

### 🏆 **"Microbots: Lightweight AI Agents for Safe and Controlled Code Automation"**

**Why this works:**
- Clearly identifies the technology (Microbots, AI Agents)
- Highlights key benefits (Lightweight, Safe, Controlled)
- Specifies the domain (Code Automation)
- Appeals to both technical and management audiences
- Length is appropriate for conference programs (62 characters)

## Title Selection Guidelines

When choosing your final title, consider:

1. **Audience**: Who will be attending? Developers, architects, security professionals, or executives?
2. **Conference theme**: Does the event have specific tracks or themes to align with?
3. **Time allocation**: Longer presentations can have more detailed titles
4. **Differentiation**: What makes your talk unique compared to other AI/LLM talks?
